1. Look at the in-game dial and identify the 3 left and 3 right symbols.
  2. Click each slot on the left and right to assign those symbols.
  3. Click a symbol again to toggle its illumination (lit or not).
  4. Once all 6 slots are filled and symbols have been toggled, click Lock In.
  5. The map will show which symbols you need to interact with to solve the puzzle.
  6. Click Reset to start over.
